At a glance

The Castle and Park of Grazzano Visconti can be visited with guided tours on weekends, public holidays and days before public holidays, with different itineraries dedicated to the castle, the park and the village. The village is always open and can be visited freely, while the castle and park can only be visited with a guide and ticket, preferably purchased online.

Types of tour

All visits take place only with a guide and include tours of the castle, park, village or combinations of these, with different durations.

Guided tour of the castle

Duration about 60 minutes, with a route through the great hall, main historical rooms and interiors of the manor, enriched with stories about the family and the history of the place.

This tour is not suitable for children under 6 years and the castle is not accessible to visitors with reduced mobility; pets are not allowed inside.

Park tour

Approximate duration 45 minutes, with a walk in the historic park, stops at panoramic points and insights into the main plant species and the history of the garden.

Castle + Park tour

Combined itinerary (about 1 hour 45 minutes) that joins the castle interiors with the guided walk in the park, for a complete visit to the historic complex.

Village + Park tour

Duration about 1 hour 30 minutes, with a route through the village and park, mixing architecture, evocative views and historical notes on the creation of the art village.

One-hour overview tour designed to give a complete first impression: it includes the village, ground floor of the castle with inner courtyard and selected viewpoints in the park.
