At a glance

Grazzano Visconti is located about 12 km south-east of Piacenza, along the Nure Valley, and is served by two large paid car parks (North and South), suitable also for coaches and camper vans.

The village is always freely accessible, while the castle and park can only be visited with guided tours during opening periods.

General information

Village

The village is always open and freely accessible, with no opening or closing times.

The area is accessible to people with disabilities and pets are allowed, provided they are kept on a leash and owners have waste bags with them.

Castle and park

The castle and the park can only be visited with a paid guided tour during opening periods.

For full information on dates, times, and ticket purchase, see the "Visits" page on the official website, where online booking is recommended.

Parking and stopping

The village car parks have a daily rate (24 hours) and accommodate cars, motorbikes, camper vans, and coaches; they are not equipped as camping areas.

There is a free parking area reserved for vehicles transporting people with disabilities, located near the South car park.
